Who is Dr. Brian Boxer Wachler?


Inventor, Author, Father, and Husband

Dr. Boxer Wachler is known as America’s TV Eye Doctor™. He has appeared on numerous television programs such as The Doctor’s Show, EXTRA, The Today Show, Good Day L.A, NBC, FOX, CNN, ABC, etc.


Professional Experience:

  • Boxer Wachler Vision Institute, Director (present position)
  • UCLA Laser Refractive Center, Director
  • Refractive Surgery, Jules Stein Institute, Director
  • Refractive Surgery Resident & Fellowship Training, Jules Stein Institute
  • Refractive Surgery Clinic, University of Kansas Medical Center, Director

Education and Training:

  • University of Kansas Medical Center, Fellowship in Refractive & Corneal Surgery
  • Saint Louis University Eye Institute, Residency in Ophthalmology
  • Dartmouth Medical School, New Hampshire, Doctor of Medicine
  • Edinburgh University, Scotland, Rotary Scholar
  • University of California, Los Angeles, Bachelor of Science in Psychobiology

Regarded as “The Doctor’s Doctor” often consulted by other eye surgeons who need assistance with challenging patients. His peers come to him when they need eye surgery performed on their own eyes.

He is trusted by patients, colleagues, and by the state and federal government. He has been trusted as an FDA investigator in 5 clinical trials, and honored by the United States Army and Department of Defense.
